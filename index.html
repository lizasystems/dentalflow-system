📋 Patient Flow

Every conversation follows these 6 steps. Nothing stays open due to disorganization.

📥

1. Patient Entry

The patient contacts the clinic through any active channel (WhatsApp, DM, web form).

⚡

2. Initial Response — Under 5 Minutes

An immediate, clear, and human response is sent. Use the Initial Message template.

🎯

3. Basic Qualification

Short questions to understand treatment type, interest level, and urgency. Max 2 questions at a time.

🔄

4. Structured Follow-Up

No response? Send follow-up at 24 hours, then again at 48 hours. Maximum 2 attempts.

📅

5. Move to Scheduling

Patient shows interest or asks to book → hand off to your team. Stop structured responses here.

🔒

6. Cycle Closure

No response after both follow-ups? Close respectfully. If they return, the conversation re-enters the system.

Every conversation ends in one of three outcomes:
✅ Moves to appointment scheduling
🔄 Remains in structured follow-up
🔒 Respectfully closed

📅 Move to Appointment Scheduling

🔒 Cycle Closure
For now, we'll close this follow-up. If you need information in the future, we'll be happy to assist.
⚠️ Remember

Never send the 48-hour follow-up without sending the 24-hour one first. Maximum 2 follow-up attempts. After closure, do not reach out again unless the patient contacts you first.
